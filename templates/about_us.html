Adoptly
Adoptly is a pet adoption tech start up where we help find pets rescued from different harmful enviroments a new home. Right now Adoptly focuses mostly on dogs, cats and porcupines. Once a pet has been adopted, it is listed as not available for adoption. We are a Canadian start up and work mostly out of Alberta, Canada and would love to grow to be a worldwide organization finding endangered pets a new home!
